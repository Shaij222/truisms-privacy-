Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026  ·  Developer: Shai Wagner

Overview

Truisms Over Drinks: Happy Hour Edition ("the App") is a conversation card game. This policy explains what information the App collects and how it's used.

Information We Collect

  • Purchase information: If you unlock premium content (the Vault), payment is processed entirely by Apple's App Store or Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment card details.
  • Gameplay data: Category selections, depth settings, and game progress may be stored locally on your device to improve your experience. This data is not transmitted to our servers.
  • No account required: The App does not require account creation, login, or personal identifying information to play.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, location, contacts, or photos unless you explicitly choose to share content (for example, screenshotting a prompt to share elsewhere — that action is outside the App's control).

Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store / Google Play: Handle all in-app purchase transactions under their own privacy policies.
  • Analytics: Should we add anonymous usage analytics in future versions, this policy will be updated first.

Children's Privacy

This App is intended for adult audiences (17+) due to mature conversational themes. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Data Security

Any data stored locally on your device is protected by your device's own security features. We do not maintain external servers storing personal user data at this time.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will be revised and, where required, users will be notified via an app update.
